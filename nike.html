NIKE

Nike is a global athletic footwear and apparel brand that has become synonymous with innovation, performance, and style. Founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has grown into one of the world's leading sportswear companies, known for its iconic swoosh logo and timeless slogan, "Just Do It." The brand's success is built on a commitment to pushing boundaries, both in product design and in promoting an active lifestyle. Through partnerships with athletes and cultural influencers, as well as a focus on sustainability and social responsibility, Nike continues to inspire and empower athletes of all levels to reach their full potential.
