Amiri brand: Where luxury meets rebellion in every stitch..

Amiri is a luxury fashion brand founded by designer Mike Amiri in Los Angeles in 2014. Known for its edgy, rock 'n' roll aesthetic, Amiri blends high-end craftsmanship with streetwear influences to create bold and distinctive clothing and accessories. The brand gained rapid recognition for its signature distressed denim, leather jackets, and graphic tees, which exude a sense of effortless coolness and rebellious attitude. Each piece is meticulously crafted with attention to detail, often featuring hand-applied treatments and embellishments that give it a unique, lived-in feel.

Amiri's collections draw inspiration from various subcultures, music, and art, reflecting a fusion of luxury and street culture. The brand's designs cater to a discerning clientele seeking luxury fashion with an edge, making it a favorite among celebrities, musicians, and fashion influencers worldwide. In addition to its ready-to-wear collections, Amiri has expanded its offerings to include accessories such as footwear, bags, and jewelry, all designed with the same level of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Overall, Amiri embodies a spirit of individuality and self-expression, offering luxury fashion for those who dare to stand out from the crowd and embrace their unique sense of style.
